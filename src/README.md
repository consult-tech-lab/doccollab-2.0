# DocCollab 2.0 — Demo Prototype

> **Secure clinical collaboration, reimagined.**

---

## What is DocCollab?

DocCollab is a secure, cloud-based platform built specifically for healthcare teams. It brings doctors, nurses, pharmacists, and other care team members into one shared digital space — so everyone stays on the same page during patient care, whether they're in the same building or working remotely.

Think of it as a **collaboration hub for clinicians** — where patient notes, real-time updates, AI assistance, and clinical decision tools all live together in one HIPAA-focused environment.

---

## Who is it for?

- **Attending Physicians & Residents** running morning rounds or managing complex cases
- **Nurses & Charge Nurses** who need to document updates and coordinate care quickly
- **Pharmacists** reviewing medications and flagging interactions
- **Social Workers & Case Managers** planning discharges and coordinating services
- **Physical Therapists & Allied Health** contributing to the interdisciplinary team
- **Remote clinicians** who need to participate in rounds from anywhere

---

## What can you do with DocCollab?

### 🏥 Clinical Workspace
The heart of the app. Open a patient's chart and see everything at a glance — vitals, medications, lab results, clinical notes, and the care plan — all in one place. Switch between patients instantly from a sidebar list, and see their acuity level, room number, and key vitals before you even open their chart.

### 📝 Rounds Notes
During patient rounds, any team member can jot a quick note right inside the patient chart. Choose the type of update (Follow-Up, Coordination, Critical Update, Discharge Planning, etc.), set a priority level (Routine to Critical), and tag yourself as the author. Notes are timestamped and immediately visible to the whole care team.

### 📋 Rounds Transcript
A shared, running feed of every note submitted during rounds — across all patients. The whole team can see what's been documented, search by patient name or MRN, filter by payer, room, or note type, and sort by newest or oldest. It's like a live logbook for the entire unit, visible to everyone credentialed to view it.

### 🎤 Ambient AI Scribe
Just talk. DocCollab's Ambient AI listens during a clinical encounter and automatically drafts a structured SOAP note (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan) — the standard format clinicians use for documentation. It also suggests relevant ICD-10 billing codes. You review it, approve it, and it's ready to go. No more typing notes after a long shift.

### 🗂️ Notes Vault
Every SOAP note generated by the Ambient AI is automatically saved here with a timestamp and session label. You can review past sessions, expand individual notes to read the full documentation, copy notes to your clipboard, or delete ones you no longer need.

### 🔬 Evidence Tools
Quick access to the clinical decision support tools your team already uses — InterQual, MCG guidelines, and CMS coverage lookups — all linked in one place. In future versions, these tools will connect directly to the patient's data so criteria checks happen automatically.

### 💬 Live Session Room
A secure, real-time messaging space for the care team during rounds. Share updates, attach files, tag topics with hashtags, and communicate — all within an end-to-end encrypted environment that logs every message for HIPAA audit purposes.

### 📱 Multi-Device Support
DocCollab works on phones, tablets, wearables, and desktops. Vitals from an Apple Watch? They sync right in. Nurses can document from bedside on a tablet; physicians can review charts on a laptop across the floor.

### 🎓 Training Module
New to DocCollab? The built-in Training section walks you through everything — from getting oriented, to understanding security, to using the AI scribe — with short lessons and quick knowledge checks.

## Security & Compliance

DocCollab is built with HIPAA compliance at its core:

- All messages and notes are **end-to-end encrypted**
- Every action is logged in an **immutable audit trail**
- Data is protected with **AES-256 encryption**
- Sessions are secured with **TLS 1.3**
- Designed for **Business Associate Agreement (BAA)** readiness

Patient data never leaves the secure environment. You can see the encryption status live in the workspace header at all times.
